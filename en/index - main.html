How do I get rated?

Download the JuniStat app and get registered

The player or his representative download a free application from the App Store or Google Play and goes through a simple registration process

Choose from 1 to 3 coaches and pay for the player review and assessment

A football player can pay for the viewing himself or he can send a payment link to his representative

Get video instructions, perform the tests and upload results to the app

The football player performs 3 tests in front of the phone’s camera. Every test comes with detailed tutorials and video-example

You’ll get your international JuniStat rating
and coaches’ recommendations in a week

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I be sure that my kid is reviewed by real coaches?

    The Coaching licenses can be provided at your request. They contain all the data on the coach you are interested in . All our coaches are licensed and employed by the leading European football clubs. Besides that, most of our coaches are authors of published research and methodological papers that can be easily found on the internet via search engines using appropriate keywords.

  • How reliable is the JuniStat rating?

    Currently, the rating is a newly-developed method that allows us to perform an objective assessment of the child’s football skills and provide him with individual development recommendations. The project team is working hard to gain international recognition for the JuniStat rating.

  • I’m 6 years old. Can I perform all the tests by myself?

    Yes, you can. The tests are made in a way that allows any football player of your age to complete them. You just need to believe in yourself. And you’ll also need some assistance from your parents or your friends to help you record the exercises on your phone’s camera.

  • I got the lowest rating in my age group. What should I do?

    First, the rating you’ve received already comes with recommendations on what you should do and what exactly you should work on to improve your game.

    Second, after following the recommendations you can retake the tests in 2-3 months and it is most likely that your skills will be rated higher.

    Third, your rating is based on you. Train and get better, and we will help you!

  • We want to get a review from CSKA or Spartak. Is this possible?

    You should contact the Children's Academies of these clubs. JuniStat currently works only with coaches from European clubs and academies.

  • Do I have a real chance of playing in Europe or England if I’ll go for an internship?

    Everything depends on how you’ll prove yourself during the internship. Anyway, this is a real chance for you to show your abilities.
